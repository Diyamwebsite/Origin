ICM KEY POINTS:
1.Motivational Tool
Incentive programs motivate employees to push and challenge themselves to achieve higher degrees of productivity

2.Promoting Teamwork
Incentive plans tied to teamwork or group initiatives can help promote collaborative work efforts in your business. 

3.Morale Boosters
Incentive plans have the potential to raise morale and increase job satisfaction in a company. 

4.Service Levels
Employees competing for or striving to meet the goals of an incentive plan may provide higher degrees of service to your customers.

5.Incentive Program Essentials
For an incentive program to be effective, goals and objectives should be clearly defined for staffers. 

6.Higher sales
Improving sales numbers is perhaps the most common reason executives seek out incentive compensation management plans. 

7.Teamwork
Many people view sales as a zero-sum game in which employees compete against one another for top clients and larger paychecks.

8.Increased engagement
A recent Gallup poll found that 70 percent of Americans are disengaged from their work. 

9.Reduced turnover
When an employee is satisfied with their compensation plan - whether it includes base pay plus commission, bonus rates or merit increases - they are more likely to stay loyal to a company.

10.Improved morale
A happy worker is an efficient one, as well as a positive influence on the rest of the office.
